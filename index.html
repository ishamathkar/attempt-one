Indian Cinema

Indian cinema, known as Bollywood, is a vibrant industry dating back to the early 20th century. It features diverse genres, colorful musicals, and melodramatic storytelling, captivating audiences worldwide. Regional film industries like Tollywood and Kollywood add to its richness, reflecting India's cultural diversity. Indian cinema serves as a powerful medium for social commentary and cultural expression, blending tradition with modernity on a global stage.

Legendary filmmakers like Satyajit Ray and contemporary directors such as Mani Ratnam have shaped Indian cinema's trajectory, while actors like Amitabh Bachchan and Shah Rukh Khan have achieved international acclaim, making Bollywood a cultural ambassador for India's rich artistic heritage.

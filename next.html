Bollywood

Bollywood refers to the Hindi-language film industry based in Mumbai (formerly Bombay), India. It is one of the largest film industries in the world, producing a significant number of films annually. The term "Bollywood" is a portmanteau of Bombay (the former name of Mumbai) and Hollywood, the iconic American film industry. Bollywood films are known for their colorful sets, elaborate dance sequences, melodramatic storylines, and often feature catchy musical numbers. These films encompass a wide range of genres including romance, comedy, action, drama, and more. Bollywood movies have a vast audience not only in India but also in many other parts of the world, especially among the Indian diaspora.

Over the years, Bollywood has evolved significantly, embracing new technologies and storytelling techniques. It has also gained international recognition with some films being showcased at major film festivals and receiving critical acclaim worldwide. Some notable Bollywood actors include Amitabh Bachchan, Shah Rukh Khan, Aamir Khan, Salman Khan, and actresses like Madhuri Dixit, Deepika Padukone, and Priyanka Chopra, among others.
